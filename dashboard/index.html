04

So what? Shift the support model

The data points one way: academic metrics alone miss the students most at risk.

REC 01

Protect study time

Study hours are the dominant lever on performance. Support that protects focused study time will move outcomes more than any single wellbeing perk.

REC 02

Close the treatment gap

Distress is widespread but help-seeking is rare. Low-barrier, opt-out-style check-ins reach students who would never book an appointment.

REC 03

Look beyond CGPA

The strongest depression predictors are personal circumstances, not grades. Screening on academic performance alone misses the at-risk group.
